About Fitness First

Abuja's premier fitness destination — built for women who want to be stronger, healthier, and more confident.

Our Story

Fitness First was founded with one simple belief — that every woman deserves access to a world-class fitness environment right here in Abuja. We started as a small studio in Wuse 2 and have grown into one of the most recognised gym brands in the FCT.

We offer a welcoming, results-driven space where members of all fitness levels can train with confidence. From cardio and strength training to yoga and kickboxing — we have something for everyone.
